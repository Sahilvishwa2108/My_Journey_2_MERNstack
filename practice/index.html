    Our Success Story

    Jagran Social Welfare Society, Bhopal stands proud with more than 25 years of excellence in the field of education.

    With DPS Bhopal’s inception in 2000, JSWS changed the pattern of education from a convent school to a public school within MP. It just not brought about the change in the outlook but has also proved itself in these years with consistent performances in Board Examinations, excellence in sports and cultural activities. read more >>
